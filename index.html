Rokda is Jugaad's in-server virtual currency. It has no real-world monetary value, cannot be exchanged for real money, cryptocurrency, or goods, and cannot be traded between servers. All gambling mechanics in Jugaad are for entertainment inside Discord only.

02

What it does

💰

Economy & Jobs

Earn Rokda through daily rewards, weekly claims, and a job system with payouts that scale as you go.

🎲

Gambling Games

Blackjack, coinflips, slots, dice, and more. All for fun, all virtual, none of it real money.

💬

Fun & Social Commands

Truth or dare, roasts, compliments, ship compatibility, and other ways to keep a server lively.

🖼️

Image & Meme Tools

Turn avatars into wanted posters, deep-fried memes, ASCII art, and other shareable nonsense.

🛡️

Moderation Suite

Locking, warnings, timeouts, and the other basics a server needs to stay in order.

📈

Community & Engagement Tools

Leveling, polls, giveaways, reaction roles, and other ways to keep members coming back.
