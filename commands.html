Economy & Jobs

Check your or someone else's Rokda balance.

Creates your wallet on first use.

/daily

Claim your daily Rokda reward.

150–400 Rokda. 24-hour cooldown. Consecutive-day streak bonus at streak ≥ 7.

/weekly

Claim your weekly Rokda reward.

1000–2000 Rokda. 7-day cooldown.

/job list

List every available job.

/job apply <name>

Apply for a job.

Dropdown of available jobs, not free text.

/job stats

View your job stats.

Per-job cooldown. Payout scales with XP.

Gambling

All bets have a min of 10 and max of 250,000 Rokda unless otherwise stated. Randomness is server-side and cryptographic.

/coinflip <amount> <side>

Flip a coin — call it right to double your bet.

Side choices: heads or tails.

/slots <amount>

Spin the reels and chase the 💎 jackpot.

3-reel emoji slots with a weighted symbol table.

/blackjack <amount>

Play a hand of blackjack against the dealer.

Hit/Stand buttons, 60-second timeout. Blackjack pays 3:2. Only one hand per user at a time.

/dice <amount> <mode> <number>

Roll a hidden d20 — guess higher/lower or the exact number.

Mode choices: higher, lower, or exact. Number is 1–20. Higher/lower pays even money, an exact guess pays a bigger multiplier.

/roulette <amount> <bet_type> [value]

Spin the wheel — red/black, odd/even, a number, or a dozen.

Bet-type choices: red, black, odd, even (all 1:1), number (35:1), dozen (2:1). Provide value as a number 0–36 for a straight-up bet, or 1/2/3 for a dozen.

/crash <amount>

Cash out before the multiplier crashes.

Multiplier climbs from 1.00x. Hit the Cash Out button before it busts.

/lottery buy <tickets>

Buy tickets for the weekly lottery draw.

The draw runs every Sunday at 20:00 UTC. Winner takes most of the pool.

/race bet <amount> <horse>

Bet on one of five emoji horses.

Opens a betting round; anyone can join before it starts.

/race start

Close betting and run the race.

Animates the finish, then pays out the winning horse's backers. Unstarted rounds auto-cancel and refund.

/elimination start

Start a last-one-standing event.

React to join within the entry window. One entrant is eliminated at a time until one remains and takes the pot.

/elimination config <enabled>

Configure whether eliminated players get a short timeout.

Requires the Manage Server permission.

Moderation

Moderation commands are gated by real Discord permissions — Discord hides each one from anyone who lacks the matching permission, so most members never see this category at all. Every action here (except /slowmode, /lock, and /unlock, which reply publicly since they change visible channel state) replies privately to the moderator, and is also posted to the server's mod-log channel if one is configured with /modlog set.

/warn <user> <reason>

Warn a member.

Requires Moderate Members. Auto-times the member out for 10 minutes on their 3rd (or later) warning, if role hierarchy allows it.

/warnings <user>

List a member's warning history.

Requires Moderate Members. Shows the 15 most recent, each with reason, moderator, and timestamp.

/kick <user> [reason]

Kick a member from the server.

Requires Kick Members.

/ban <user> [reason]

Ban a member from the server.

Requires Ban Members. Works even if the user isn't currently in the server.

/unban <user-id> [reason]

Unban a user by their ID.

Requires Ban Members. Checks that the user is actually banned first, so the reply is unambiguous either way.

/timeout <user> <duration> [reason]

Time a member out.

Requires Moderate Members. Duration is in minutes, capped at 40,320 (Discord's 28-day timeout limit).

/purge <amount>

Bulk-delete recent messages in the current channel.

Requires Manage Messages. Capped at 100 per run — Discord's bulk-delete limit. Messages older than 14 days are silently skipped and reported as such.

/slowmode <seconds>

Set this channel's slowmode, or disable it.

Requires Manage Channels. 0–21,600 seconds (Discord's 6-hour cap). Use 0 to disable.

/lock [reason]

Lock the current channel — stops @everyone from sending messages.

Requires Manage Channels.

/unlock [reason]

Unlock the current channel.

Requires Manage Channels. Resets the permission to inherit rather than force-allowing, so category/role overrides still apply.

/lockdown [reason]

Raid response — lock every text/announcement/forum channel in the server.

Requires Manage Channels. Shows a Confirm/Cancel prompt (30s, scoped to you) before touching anything.

/unlockdown [reason]

Lift a server-wide lockdown — unlock every text/announcement/forum channel.

Requires Manage Channels. Same Confirm/Cancel prompt as /lockdown.

/nickname reset <user>

Clear a member's nickname.

Requires Manage Nicknames.

/modlog set <channel>

Set the channel where moderation actions are logged.

Requires Manage Server. Checks the bot can actually post there before saving.
